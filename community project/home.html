Growing Guides

Learn how to grow these popular plants in small spaces

Tomatoes

Tomatoes are perfect for container gardening and can thrive on balconies with 6-8 hours of sunlight. Use a 5-gallon pot with drainage holes, quality potting mix, and water consistently.

Mint

Mint grows vigorously in small spaces and containers. It prefers partial shade and regular watering. Keep mint in its own container as it can spread aggressively.

Curry Leaves

Curry leaf plants thrive in warm conditions with bright, indirect light. Use well-draining soil and water when the top inch feels dry. Harvest leaves as needed for cooking.
